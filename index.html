mediClinic

A medical application for all university students with different specialties and for academic staff to provide health services With daily health advice..

Mediclinic is the easiest website that saves your time! The idea of the site came because we are students at the University of Palestine, and whenever we want to go to the university clinics, we take a long time to wait in addition to the crowding, so visiting the medical clinic has become a heavy burden for the patient, and from here we suggested that you have a website and an application that helps in booking your appropriate appointment with the right doctor. All clinics are connected to their facilities like pharmacy, medical laboratories and radiology departments. To facilitate receiving medical services and maintaining better health
